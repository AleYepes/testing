Hot Chocolate

A cozy drink to warm you up on a cold day.

Ingredients:

Serves four.

  • 5 cloves
  • 4 tablets of Lucker Classic chocolate
  • 1L milk
  • 2 tbsp of cinnamon
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • enough sugar to make your heart sing

Procedure:

  1. In a mortar and pestel, crush the cloves into a powder.
  2. Mix the ingredients in a pot.
  3. Bring the solution to a boil. (Beware! milk boils over quickly)
